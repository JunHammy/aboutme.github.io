Sketching As A Hobby

Pencil sketching have become more than just a regular hobby for me; it serves as a gateway to self-expression. Sketching allows me to break free from the limits of reality, to explore uncharted and unbounded areas. Being one who prefers portraiture, I enjoy sketching various Anime charaters. Additionally, my sketchbook holds a collection of intricate portrait sketches. With each pencil line and shading, I aim to tell a story and convey the emotions of each unique individual. With a pecil and paper, I find a profound connection with the world around me.

My Best Artwork

Broken skeleton

The artwork portrays a skeleton clutching a broken heart, with its eyes shedding blood tears. It symbolizes profound loss and heartache. The skeleton represents mortality, while the shattered heart represents emotional distress. The dark tones and contrasting colors set a somber mood, with the shades of red intensifying the anguish. This artwork depicts the narrative of the sorrow and grief of an individual who poured their entire being into a pursuit, only to find themselves wounded and their heart fragmented.
