School of Information technology | Diploma in Information Technology

What is is about?

The Diploma in Information Technology offers an opportunity to contribute to a better world through the use of technologies such as artificial intelligence and cloud computing. This program exposes you to various facets of IT, enabling you to comprehend the underlying mechanisms that make these achievements possible. Gain proficiency in developing groundbreaking software solutions and take your first stride toward a highly sought-after profession in artificial intelligence or cloud computing.

Why did i select this diploma?

I am really passionate about technology. It fascinates me that the capability of technology is limitless. From Coding to Artificial Intelligence to emerging tech trends, I am always eager to endeavour and expapand my knowledge. I believe that with the power of technology, I will be able to bring positive changes and better the life of others.
